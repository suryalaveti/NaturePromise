Overview

This initiative seeks to transform agriculture by using machine learning to prioritize sustainable crop choices. By inputting soil data, farmers receive personalized recommendations for crops and fertilizers, fostering informed decision-making and advancing technology-driven, sustainable farming practices.

Parameters

Based on the requirements like which kind of soil,weather is best suited for growing a particular crop,what will be the amount of water,N,P,K required for that crop to be grown,what will be the PH for that crop,in what quantity must the chemicals of the soil must be in,such that the crop can sustain more life and has no harm from threats,all these parameters are taken into consideration for building the model,which takes up these parameters and predicts the best type of crop/fruit that the farmer must grow
