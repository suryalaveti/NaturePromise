Data Visuals For Production of Crops

We can observe the trend of Total Production of several widely used crops/fruits StateWise, below visuals display the stats for the top 5 states which produce the crop/fruit, which are having high impact on the Production

Observations:

- The production of Rice has been consistently increasing over the years in states like West Bengal and Uttar Pradesh.
- Jute production shows a declining trend in recent years, especially in West Bengal.
- Maize production has seen a significant increase in states like Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh.
- Mango production has remained stable over the years, with Maharashtra being the leading producer.
- Papaya production has been fluctuating, with Uttar Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh being the major producers.
